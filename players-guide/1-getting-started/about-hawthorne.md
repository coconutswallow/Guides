## Welcome to Hawthorne!

Hawthorne is a thriving West Marches-style D&D community that brings together players and Dungeon Masters from around the world. Our server offers a unique blend of organized play and creative freedom.

![Hawthorne Banner](/assets/images/hawthorne-banner.png)

## What Makes Hawthorne Special?

### West Marches Style

Hawthorne follows the West Marches format, which means:

- **Player-Driven**: Players organize their own groups and choose their adventures
- **Persistent World**: The world continues to evolve based on player actions
- **Flexible Scheduling**: Play when you want with whoever's available
- **Shared Progress**: Your character progresses across multiple sessions

### Community Features

- **Active Discord Server**: Connect with hundreds of players and DMs
- **Multiple DMs**: Choose from various DM styles and campaign themes
- **Downtime System**: Engage with the world between sessions
- **Custom Content**: Unique monsters, items, and lore created by the community

## The Setting

Hawthorne is set in a rich fantasy world with diverse regions to explore:

### The Split Tooth Mountains

A treacherous mountain range filled with ancient ruins and dangerous creatures.

### The Feywild Domains

Mysterious domains ruled by powerful Archfey, each with their own unique characteristics.

### The Region of Dreams

A surreal landscape where reality and imagination blend together.
