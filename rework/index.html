Cost Calculations

▼ Cost Information

Costs are based on the type of change:

  • 2024 Update Rework (0 cost)
  • Any rework before level 6 (0 cost)
  • Checkpoint Rework (cost based on tier)
  • Story Rework (cost based on tier)
  • A-la-carte Rework (cost on a per-change basis)
    • Changing 1 class level, species, background, or feat/ASI: 1 change each
    • Changing language/tool/skill proficiency: 1 change
    • Changing subclass: 1 change per class level with subclass features
    • Changing starting ability scores or character identity: 2 changes
    • 2014 to 2024 (or vice versa): 1 change (must switch all options to equivalent)
Note: These calculations are a draft. It is your responsibility to ensure accuracy. If you need help, copy the Rework ID and ping an Auditor.
