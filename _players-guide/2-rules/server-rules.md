## General Conduct

All members of the Hawthorne D&D Guild are expected to maintain a respectful and inclusive environment.

### Respect Everyone

- Treat all members with courtesy and respect
- No harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Respect different playstyles and preferences

### Communication Guidelines

- Keep discussions on-topic in designated channels
- Use appropriate language
- Resolve conflicts privately or with moderator assistance

## Gaming Rules

### Session Scheduling

Games are scheduled through the dedicated scheduling channels. Please:

1. RSVP promptly when invited to a game
2. Notify the DM at least 24 hours in advance if you cannot attend
3. Arrive on time for scheduled sessions

### Character Creation

All characters must follow these guidelines:

- Use official sources or approved homebrew only
- Submit characters for approval before first session
- Maintain appropriate power levels for the campaign

## Content Restrictions

### Allowed Content

- Official D&D 5e sourcebooks (2014 & 2024)
- Approved homebrew content
- Unearthed Arcana (with DM permission)

### Prohibited Content

- Overly sexual or graphic content
- Content that promotes real-world hate or violence
- Disruptive or "joke" characters

## Consequences

Violations of these rules may result in:

1. **First offense**: Warning from moderators
2. **Second offense**: Temporary suspension
3. **Serious violations**: Permanent ban
